Home - We Are Their Defence

Our Mission is to provide unwavering support, resources, and a sense of community to Australian Defence Force veterans, honoring their service and empowering them as they transition to civilian life. We strive to be a trusted source of support, guidance, and pportunitity for veterans as they embark on their post-service journey.

Origin - Beginning of Veteran Defence

Inspired by a deep sense of gratitude and a profound respect for the men and women who have served our nation, John Badman, a dedicated veteran and compassionate leader, founded Veteran Defence to make a meaningful difference in the lives of our military heroes. Having witnessed the challenges and sacrifices firsthand, John Badman embarked on a mission to ensure that no veteran would ever feel alone or unsupported. He believed that through collective efforts and unwavering dedication, we could honor their service and provide the vital support they so rightfully deserve. Veteran Defence stands as a testament to John Badman's commitment to giving back and making a lasting impact on the lives of Australia's defenders.

    November 20 - 11 AM

    November 31 - 10 AM

    Skilled tradespeople and volunteers come together to renovate and improve the homes of ADF veterans. We aims to create a safe and comfortable living spaces for those who've defended our country.

Support

Just like our founder, Veteran Defence strives to create a country where every soldier who put their life on the line can be supported by the people they protected. Veteran Defence provides support to these soldiers, ranging from medical and financial aid to mental and physical relief. Thanks to affiliates and sponsors, Veteran Defence can even provide assistance in finding employment and housing. If you served in the ADF and are in need of assistance, click below.
