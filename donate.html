Veteran Defence Trust Fund

Donations to the Veteran Defence Trust Fund allow our organization to provide life-changing support to thousands of disabled military servicemen and women. Donations to the Trust Fund allow us to continue operating at maximum capacity, and help provide medical, financial and psychological aid to ex-servicepeople
